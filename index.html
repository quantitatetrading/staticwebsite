Rapidly Deploy Solutions to Optimize Your Data Workflows

The Quantitate Platform has been designed from the ground up to suit the needs of high frequency trading. It's heavily optimized C backend allows it to rapidly ingest and process data from numerous sources, simultaneously, in real-time. All this while being easy to slot into pre-existing data infrastructure, allowing for a quick and painless deployment.

Rapid Prototyping

While the highly efficient backend of the Quantitate Platform ensures rapid backtesting execution speed, the development process itself is designed to be as fast and painless as possible. The front-end is designed to work in familiar python IDEs and there exist numerous integrations for existing back-testing software.
